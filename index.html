Natural Disasters and Precautions

CONNECTING PEOPLE, SAVING LIVES

Why Disaster Management?

India is one of the most disaster-prone countries in the world. Every year millions of Indians are affected by natural disasters...

Earthquake

Description: Earthquakes are sudden and violent shaking of the ground, caused by movement of tectonic plates...

Precautions: Drop, Cover, and Hold On. Stay indoors, away from windows, and protect your head.

Flood

Description: Floods occur when water levels rise above normal, inundating areas that are usually dry...

Precautions: Move to higher ground. Avoid walking or driving through flooded areas.

Hurricane

Description: A hurricane is a powerful tropical storm with strong winds and heavy rain...

Precautions: Stay indoors in a sturdy building, away from windows. Keep emergency supplies on hand.

Wildfire

Description: A wildfire is an uncontrolled and unpredictable fire in an area of combustible vegetation...

Precautions: Evacuate early if advised. Stay in a safe, cleared area, and avoid breathing smoke.
